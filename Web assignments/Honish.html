Tips to learn Quickly


Tip 1

Work on your fundamentals for the programming language of your choice. The fundamentals are largely the same across languages (variables, functions, conditional statements (if-else, while loops, for loops), data structures (arrays, objects)). Once you pick up the fundamentals for one language, the process for learning another language can be quite similar. Sharpen your fundamentals by doing programming challenges (do one a day if you can). The fundamentals will go a long way when you’re building your app.


Tip 2

Fixing bugs and errors. We will undoubtedly see error messages when we’re learning to code. They are scary at first, but they are in fact our best friends.

Tip 3

Build small projects. Build something in one afternoon, a weekend, or a week. Even though simple projects like Hello World projects, a blog, or a twitter clone may seem trivial, they are a great way for us to build something functional and to gain confidence! They are scary at first, but they are in fact our best friends.
